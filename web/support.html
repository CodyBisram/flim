Common questions

How do I get in? FLIM says I'm not invited.

FLIM is invite-only right now. Ask a friend who's already on FLIM to add your email, then sign in with the code we send you.

Why do my photos take time to appear?

That's the point — FLIM shoots like a disposable camera. Your photos "develop" over a short window before they appear in your Darkroom.

How do I report or block someone?

Tap the ••• menu on any photo to report it or block the person who posted it. We review reports and act on objectionable content and abusive users within 24 hours. See our Terms for details.

How do I delete my account?

Open your Profile, scroll down, and tap Delete Account. This permanently removes your account, photos, posts, and rolls. It can't be undone.

How do I turn off notifications?

Open Settings from your Profile and toggle notifications off, or manage them in your device's iOS Settings.

Still stuck?

Email codyysb@gmail.com — include your FLIM version (shown at the bottom of Settings) so we can help faster.
